Things To Do

For out of town guests, this could be an amazing opportunities to explore some of the best experiences SF has to offer. There are SO many restaurants, bars, museums and shopping locations it's hard to narrow.

Union Square

Union Square has many malls, restaurants, and stores. There are four heart statues around the square that are great for taking places. It's very accessible by public transit and in the heart of SF.

Golden Gate Park

A large tourist attraction featuring the deYoung Museum, Academy of Sciences, and Japanese Tea Garden.

Legion of Honor and
Land's End

The Legion of Honor is a famous European art museum that is definitely worth a visit, especially for lovers of French art. Land's End is also a great hiking spot that's next to the Legion of Honor with a great view of the bay and Golden Gate bridge.

Presidio

SF is a great place to hike. The Presidio is a hiking spot that has the Walt Disney Family museum and old military buildings. At one end of the Presidio is the Golden Gate Bridge.

Napa Valley

Napa Valley is home to many famous Californian wineries. If you love wine, Napa Valley is a great place to visit for wine tasting. Consider booking a wine tour in advance.

Berkeley

Berkeley is an important cultural and intellectual center. It's home to UC Berkeley, one of the world's finest institutions of higher education. The food and people here are as interesting as you've heard.
